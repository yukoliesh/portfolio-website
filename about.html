My Story

Hi, I'm Yuko, a UI/UX designer with a strong Front End Development, Web and Graphic Design background.

I worked at the last company (Cornerstone OnDemand) as a Front End Developer for several years. I gained a lot of experience by building various HR Performance related features (React) including setting up, maintaining, and testing accessibility functionalities. I also was involved in the design process with other product designers and product managers. This whole process including accessibility, design and client feedback got me interested to think about and focus on user-centric experience design with “inclusiveness” in mind.

I thrive at problem solving. I enjoy to come up with new ideas that are outside of the box. With this mindset, changing my career path to UI/UX was a natural choice for me.

I am passionate about designing products from the user's perspective making sure that the product is also impactful for the business needs. If you would like to know more about me, let's connect at yshiozaki.tech[at]gmail.com or at LinkedIn.
